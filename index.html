About Me

I am a whimsical lover of fountain pens with a heart for great design. Life is too short for crappy user experiences. When I'm not not working on a great new project my interests include:

  • Collecting and using fountain pens
  • Finding interesting uses for bacon grease
  • Adding books to my shelf with every intention of reading them...eventually
  • Befriending random puppers and kittehs

I stumbled into the world of UX/UI after a particularly dark season in my life. I realized in early 2019 that I was tired of the work I had been doing and wanted something different for my life moving forward. After some serious soul searching, and a lot of Google searching as well, I realized I might enjoy pursuing a career in UX/UI Design. Thus began my journey into the Georgia Tech UX/UI Bootcamp starting in the Fall of 2019.
